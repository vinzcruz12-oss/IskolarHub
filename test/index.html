FAQs

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Have more questions? Get in touch

Is IskolarHub free?

▼

Yes. Students can create an account and browse scholarship opportunities completely free of charge.

Can I apply directly through IskolarHub?

▼

No. IskolarHub redirects you to the official scholarship provider where you can complete your application.

How do scholarship matches work?

▼

Click "Find My Scholarship" to open the recommendation form. After filling in your information, IskolarHub will recommend scholarships that best match your qualifications.

Will I receive deadline reminders?

▼

Yes. Saved scholarships can display upcoming deadlines to help you keep track of important application dates.

Privacy Notice & Terms of Use

What information we collect

We collect your name, email address, password, age, current school, strand, course, and general weighted average (GWA).

Why we collect this information

We collect this information to create your student account and to match you with college scholarships that fit your qualifications.

Your information is used only for scholarship recommendations and account management. We do not use your data for marketing or any other purpose.

Password security

Your password is securely stored using encryption. We never store or share your raw password.
